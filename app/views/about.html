About Weather Top Web App

This app provides you with the latest weather updates from your weather stations but also with a lot more features to give you as much information as you need.
    You are able to add stations.
    You are able to add readings.
    You are able to delete stations.
    You are able to delete readings
    View the latest readings at your stations
    View the history of your readings at your stations
